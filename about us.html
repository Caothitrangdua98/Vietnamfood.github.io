Vietnamese food

Each country has a different culinary culture, so we would like to introduce to everyone the delicious dishes of Vietnam, with the desire to exchange culinary culture with everyone in the world by the Traditional recipe easy to make.

With many delicious and strange dishes, Vietnamese cuisine is a part of the national soul, contributing to the charm of Vietnam tourism. Vietnamese people in each region have different tastes. Northern people eat lightly, mainly using fish sauce, diluted shrimp paste, many vegetable dishes and freshwater aquatic products such as shrimp, crab, fish, mussels, mussels... Hanoi cuisine is considered representative. quintessence of Northern cuisine. Central people eat spicy and salty, famous for sour shrimp paste and fish sauce. In particular, Hue people are very picky in processing and presenting dishes.
